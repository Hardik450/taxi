    • User Guidelines:


    1. Respectful Behavior:
      • Treat the driver and fellow passengers with respect and courtesy.
      • Avoid using offensive language or engaging in disruptive behavior.
    2. Seat Belts and Safety:
      • Fasten seat belts at all times during the journey, as safety is a top priority.
      • Follow any safety instructions provided by the driver.
    3. Clear Directions:
      • Provide clear and accurate directions to the driver for your destination.
      • Communicate any preferred routes or specific instructions clearly.
    4. Timeliness:
      • Be punctual for your scheduled pickup time to ensure a smooth journey.
      • Notify the driver promptly if there are any delays or changes to your plans.
    5. Cleanliness:
      • Maintain cleanliness inside the taxi by avoiding littering or leaving behind personal belongings.
      • Respect the vehicle and keep it in the same condition as when you boarded.
    6. Payment:
      • Pay the fare promptly using the accepted methods specified by the company.
      • If paying by cash, ensure you have the correct amount ready to expedite the payment process.
    7. Feedback:
      • Provide constructive feedback to the company about your experience, both positive and negative.
      • Your feedback helps us improve our services and enhance the overall passenger experience.
    8. Enjoy Your Journey:
      • Relax and enjoy your journey with us, knowing that your safety and comfort are our top priorities.
      • If you have any special requests or preferences, feel free to communicate them to the driver.
    By following these guidelines, you can contribute to a pleasant and enjoyable journey for yourself and your fellow passengers. Thank you for choosing our taxi providing company, and we look forward to serving you again in the future.
